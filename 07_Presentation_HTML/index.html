Q2

What is the theoretically fair value of gold in Egypt on any given date? And how much is the current deviation?

Fair Value vs Deviation

Fair Value Index (FVI) = Actual Price ÷ Theoretical Price - enabling real-time is now the right time to buy? signals

FVI > 1.05 = Overvalued (speculative premium exists). FVI ≈ 1.0 = Fair pricing. FVI < 0.97 = Undervalued opportunity. The index enables objective entry timing decisions.

Key Insights & Findings

💡

It's a Currency Bet, Not a Gold Bet

60-70% of Egyptian gold price movements during crises trace back to EGP depreciation, not global gold prices. USD/EGP dominates with r ≈ 0.97 correlation.

📈

Lower Costs Beat Higher Purity

21K gold outperformed 24K on net returns despite lower gold content. Transaction costs are the decisive variable - not karat purity. EGP cash suffered severe real-value destruction.

🎯

Timing Crises with Data Works

The FVI, premium %, and composite technical signal system enable objective, data-driven entry and exit timing - eliminating emotion-driven panic buying at peak premiums.

⚠️ Before: Emotion-Driven Investing

Panic-buying during crises at 15%+ premiums above fair value. No visibility into whether price rises reflect gold value or currency collapse. No systematic exit strategy.

✅ After: Intelligence-Driven Decisions

Real-time FVI signals flag overvalued entry points. Composite RSI + MACD + Bollinger Bands system automates BUY/HOLD/SELL signals. Prophet forecasts set 6-12 month price expectations.
